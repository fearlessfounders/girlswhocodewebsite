Collaborate

Learn new languages in a non-judgemental community. Build, scale - celebrate.

In our community, we cultivate the values of respect, kindness and hard work. Not only do members learn four languages - like HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Python - they also have access to networking.

There will be syntax errors. There will be times when you want to throw your computer out the window. It's probably a semicolon. We'll help you grow as a coder and a person during your time with GWC@UMD.

Empower

You'll be surrounded by people who will support you when you fail and celebrate when you get back up.

Coding is a blast. But, it's also tough. That's the reality. GWC@UMD doesn't want you to fear failure; it's inevitable and should be seen as a learning experience rather than a reflection of your character.

Hey, what'd you accomplish today on your site?
